Sara Neidorf

Drummer

lessons

I have been giving private drum lessons since 2007, with a primary focus on rock drumming, rudiments, interdependence, and polyrhythms. My teaching practice is geared towards encouraging more women, non-binary, and queer people to tackle the drums. I’m excited to work with students of all backgrounds, genders, ability levels, ages, and interests.

My rehearsal space is located in Berlin Neukölln, at Ziegrastrasse 11, near S-bahn Sonnenallee, and I am also willing to travel to students’ homes or studios.

Lessons are always flexible and tailored to each student’s particular interests and learning habits.

Contact me at booking@saraneidorf.com for more information or if you would like to set up an appointment.

Teaching Experience

  • Private instruction in the Philadelphia area and Berlin (2007-present)
  • Founder of DRUM club at Bryn Mawr College - female musicians support network with free music instruction (2009-2012)
  • Drum coach at Girls Rock Philly summer camp (2012, 2013, 2018)
  • Trash2Instrument Instrument-building Workshops and Percussion Jams (2013-2016)

Drum instruction at the following establishments

  • Neue Musikschule (2016-2017)
